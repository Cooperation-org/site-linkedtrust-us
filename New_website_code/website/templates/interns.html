LinkedTrust offers structured internship programs that provide real-world experience in software development, team collaboration, and professional growth. Our internships benefit both organizations looking to host talented interns and students seeking meaningful development experience.

For HR & Hiring Managers

Benefits of Our Interns

Our internship program prepares students with practical skills that translate directly to productive work. Interns come to you with foundational knowledge and professional habits already in place.

  • Trained in development practices: Interns understand Git workflows, pull request processes, code review etiquette, and collaborative development patterns.
  • Team collaboration skills: They know how to work in teams, communicate effectively in async and distributed environments, and contribute to shared codebases.
  • Developer relations capabilities: Interns can assist with documentation, demos, internal tooling, and small improvements that free up senior engineers for critical work.

How the Internship Works

We provide a structured onboarding process and work plan for each intern. They receive guidance on real projects but are not thrown directly into critical production systems. This approach ensures both safety and learning.

  • Comprehensive onboarding process with clear expectations and documentation
  • Structured work plans that align with your organization's needs
  • Regular check-ins and progress reviews to ensure quality and alignment
  • Gradual responsibility increase as interns demonstrate competence

Why This Is Low-Risk and High-Value

Our internship program is designed to minimize risk while maximizing value for your organization.

  • Prepared candidates: Interns arrive with foundational skills and professional habits, not starting from zero.
  • Well-scoped contributions: They work on small, clearly defined tasks that add value without risking critical systems.
  • Time savings: Interns handle documentation, demos, and incremental improvements that would otherwise consume senior engineer time.
  • Pipeline for hiring: Successful internships can serve as a pipeline for future full-time hires who already understand your codebase and culture.
