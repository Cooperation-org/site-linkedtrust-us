Frequently asked questions

How does LinkedTrust help startups cut burn rate?

We focus your spending on the product instead of overhead: migrating you off expensive managed cloud onto high-performance bare-metal or hybrid setups, implementing self-hosted tools you own rather than rent, and keeping infrastructure lean so capital goes toward growth.

What is a cloud exit strategy?

It is moving off expensive managed cloud services onto bare-metal or hybrid infrastructure. We handle the migration end to end so your app stays fast and scalable while you avoid the recurring cloud tax of egress and per-IOPS fees.

Can you replace per-seat SaaS tools like CRMs?

Yes. We implement open-source platforms such as Odoo tailored to your workflow, giving you a HubSpot-style alternative without per-seat licensing. You pay for the software, not for every employee you hire.

Do you help with founder equity?

We set up dynamic equity systems, including Slicing Pie structures, so contributions are tracked fairly and your founding team stays aligned and motivated as the company grows.

How do engagements start?

With a free call. We listen first, understand where your money and time are going, and figure out the highest-leverage fixes together before scoping any work.
