Frequently asked questions

What is an MVP and why build one?

An MVP, or minimum viable product, is the smallest production-ready version of your idea that real users can actually use. Building one gets you to market fast so you can test with real users and learn before investing in a full build.

How long does it take to build an MVP?

Weeks, not months. We build the core must-have features first, avoid scope creep and unnecessary polish, and get you a working product you can put in front of users quickly.

What technologies do you use?

We architect with clean code and scalable frameworks such as React, Next.js, and Node.js, chosen to fit your business goals and to scale as you grow.

What does the process look like?

Three phases: discovery to turn your raw idea into a technical roadmap, rapid execution to build the core features, and deployment on a production-ready environment with monitoring, analytics, and user feedback loops.

Is the MVP ready for real users at launch?

Yes. We launch on production-ready infrastructure with a focus on stability and feedback loops, and set up the monitoring and analytics needed to scale when you are ready.
