Frequently asked questions

What services does LinkedTrust offer?

We build and ship software end to end: rapid MVP development, AI knowledge assistants and integrations, bare-metal cloud migration, verifiable credentials and badges, and ongoing team augmentation. We also run tailored programs for startups and nonprofits.

How do engagements work?

Every engagement starts with a free call where we listen first and scope the work together. From there we agree on a clear deliverable and timeline, then build iteratively so you can see progress early. Pricing depends on the service — some are project-based, some monthly.

Do you work with startups and nonprofits?

Yes. We have dedicated paths for both: startups focused on cutting burn rate and owning their tools, and nonprofits building lean, high-impact systems on open-source stacks at a fraction of typical agency costs.

What is LinkedClaims?

LinkedClaims is our open standard for verifiable, linked claims — portable statements of trust, credentials, and reputation that anyone can verify. It powers the trust badges you see across our work and lets achievements and endorsements be checked independently rather than taken on faith.

What makes LinkedTrust different?

We use the tools we sell. Our own infrastructure, AI assistants, and trust graph run in production every day, and our track record is backed by verifiable LinkedClaims rather than just testimonials. You get a team that has actually shipped what it is recommending.
