Who we are

LinkedTrust is a global, employee-owned technology cooperative based in Tucson, Arizona. We build verified trust systems, civic tech platforms, and AI-powered tools for organizations that care about doing things right.

Our team spans four continents. We share equity, governance, and a belief that trust should be earned, verified, and visible — not assumed.

Every testimonial on this site is a verifiable claim on the LinkedTrust graph. That's not marketing — it's how things work.
