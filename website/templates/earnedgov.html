Democracies are losing to anonymous capital

The Liquidity Problem

Democracy (one person one vote, not transferable) is essentially a completely illiquid currency. Money is the opposite: completely transferable, not trackable, any anonymous agent can use it to purchase control over any resource. Naturally there is a tension between democratic control and anonymous control, and currently the democracies seem to be losing.

Growing as a Tree, Not a Slime Mold

When there is democratic accountability, people feel safer that their rights are protected, and they can focus on longer term goals than merely survival. These structures allow society to grow as a tree rather than as a slime mold engulfing all in its path. The question is: what kinds of structures give us the accountability of democracy with the vigorous growth that earning allows?

Builders and creators deserve ownership. Earned governance through peer-reviewed task tracking means the hardest workers gain more share, without hard feelings. Teams can grow rapidly.

How it works

Transparency-not-Permission allows leaders to drive ahead while maintaining team trust. Inclusive, fast and battle-tested shared decision making.

Task-to-Token Equity

Granular earning of governance and equity means the hardest workers gain more share, without hard feelings. Teams can grow rapidly. All work, including mentor meetings, is tracked for equity.

Transparency Not Permission

Leaders drive ahead while maintaining team trust. Visibility into decisions and resource allocation is as powerful as voting, without the delay.

Earned Leadership

CEO elected by ranked-choice, work-weighted vote. Profits spent by proposals to rotating steering committee with work-weighted random selection. Contribution-based governance prevents takeover.

Legal Equity From Day One

Real ownership via Fairmint or Slicing Pie legal instruments, not vague promises. The accelerator invests via stipends and receives shares through the Rolling SAFE.

Global, Diverse Teams

Teams are encouraged to recruit globally and stay open to adding members. Built for distributed, async collaboration. Focus on markets with high talent and low capital access where earned equity is a competitive advantage.

AI-Accelerated Building

Proof of concept is now hours not days or weeks. Integrated task tracker and AI-aligned shared dev environment, credited on the equity table as an in-kind contribution.
