Frequently asked questions

How does LinkedTrust help nonprofits?

We help mission-driven organizations build robust systems on open-source stacks at a fraction of standard agency costs, so more of your budget goes to your mission. That includes content planning, affordable custom tools, and impact tracking.

Why open-source instead of off-the-shelf software?

Open-source stacks let you own your tools rather than rent them, avoid per-seat licensing, and customize freely. For nonprofits running lean, that means professional portals, dashboards, and databases without recurring vendor lock-in.

Can you help us measure and prove our impact?

Yes. We build data visualization and reporting tools so you can track and showcase real-world results to stakeholders and grant makers. Don't just do good — prove it with verifiable data.

What is content planning for nonprofits?

It is a strategic tool that aligns your organization's mission with your digital presence, turning your long-term vision into a daily content plan that resonates with donors and supporters.
