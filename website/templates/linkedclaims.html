The LinkedClaims Ecosystem

LinkedClaims is an open, minimal standard for expressing assertions about addressable things, published at the Decentralized Identity Foundation. It enables linking to third-party sources and other claims, building a decentralized web of trust that is inclusive of external projects and data. These are the tools, specs, and platforms built on the standard.
