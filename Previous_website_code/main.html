LinkedClaims Vocabulary 1.0

Composable Claims Vocabulary includes the ability to specify a 3rd-party source, using a digest multibase hash to demonstrate that the source has not changed. This allows importation of existing web2.0 data into a durable, decentralized graph,

Partners and Protocols

LinkedTrust claims are in use in WhatCookin Communities and power discovery of Coupons for Good, a way to easily shop sustainably. They are published on ComposeDB Ceramic Streams and we are in the process of setting up a LinkedTrust-powered Oracle on the HUMAN Protocol.
